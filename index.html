Preamble

Preamble This activity was designed to help couples understand their roles, responsibilities and duties in marriage according to God's standard. It is expected that Christian couples in this group( as members of the body of Christ - the church) will exemplify God's model for marriage. In this day and age where the world is struggling with what is true and what is false, the church has to live up to our responsibility as the pillar and ground of the truth (1 Tim 3:15) We can not rely on the world or our experiences to define truth. The word of God is truth (John 17:17). If the foundation is destroyed what can the righteous do? It is our prayers that the truth of the Word of God will and cause us by His spirit to be responsible married children of God Scripture Reference 1 Timothy 3:15 But if I tarry long, that thou mayest know how thou oughtest to behave thyself in the house of God, which is the church of the living God, the pillar and ground of the truth. John 17:17 Sanctify them through thy truth: thy word is truth.

*Responsibilities*

2. Protect the wife and family
3. The man gives direction and leadership to the home
4. Love wife

6. He sacrifice for the wife and family
7. Meet the needs of the family
8. Raise the children

*Duties*
1. Be an example to the wife and children
2. Raises the children

Woman

Roles
*Roles*

1. Wife

2. Help meet

3. Home keeper

Responsibilities
*Responsibilities*
1. The wife submits to her husband
2. Meet the husband's needs which includes sex and other human needs
3. Upholds the husband in prayers and enforces directives given
4. Help raise and nurture the children (Titus 2:4-7)
5. Help to build the house.
6. Builds her home (Prov 14)
7. Reverence her husband.
Duties
*Duties*
1. The woman keeps the home and beautifies it
2. Raises the children builds her home
3. Runs the house daily
4. Brings stability to the home
5. Beautify the husband and the home
